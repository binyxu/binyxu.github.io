How One AI Assistant Solves Complex Tasks with Skill Cards

Today we are not only looking at AI chat. We will see how an assistant can work like a small research helper: reading the task, checking data, calling tools, revising its plan, and finally producing a verifiable result. These "skill cards" come from our research: From Multi-Agent to Single-Agent.

Main Idea When expert experience is written as clear skill cards, one AI assistant can handle complex tasks. The demo traces are pre-arranged so the class demonstration stays stable.
